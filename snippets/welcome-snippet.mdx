👋 Welcome to Firecrawl! 

Firecrawl is your all-in-one solution for turning websites into clean, structured data that's ready for AI and machine learning applications. Think of it as a smart website reader that can:

- 📖 **Read any webpage** - Even those tricky ones with dynamic content
- 🔄 **Convert content** - Turn messy HTML into clean markdown or structured data
- 🌐 **Explore entire sites** - Automatically find and process all related pages
- 🤖 **AI-ready output** - Get data in formats perfect for LLMs and other AI tools

Whether you're building an AI chatbot, doing market research, or just need clean data from websites, Firecrawl handles the complex parts so you can focus on building amazing things.
